Making Gifs

Making gifs is crazy fun.

From the editor, load a level, bop around in it a bit, and then press CTRL+K (or CMD+K if you're on osx), then BE PATIENT, then BAM a shiny gif ready to download should pop up in a short few moments.

It's not the fastest generator in the world, and its speed depends on the length of the animation and the resolution of the game-screen in the editor.
